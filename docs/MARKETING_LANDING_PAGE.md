### General Questions

**Q: How does the auction process work?**
A: Sellers list vehicles, buyers browse and bid. Highest bid wins. All transactions are secure and verified.

**Q: Are vehicles verified before listing?**
A: Yes, all vehicles undergo professional inspection by certified valuers and blockchain verification.

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**
A: We support multiple secure payment options including UPI, cards, net banking, and EMI options.

**Q: Is there buyer protection?**
A: Yes, we have comprehensive buyer protection including dispute resolution and refund policies.

**Q: Can I sell multiple vehicles?**
A: Yes, we offer bulk upload features and membership plans for dealers with multiple vehicles.

### For Sellers

**Q: How much commission do you charge?**
A: We have transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Commission varies by membership plan.

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Verification typically takes 24-48 hours after vehicle inspection.

**Q: Can I set a reserve price?**
A: Yes, you can set reserve prices for auctions to ensure minimum acceptable bids.

### For Buyers

**Q: Can I inspect the vehicle before buying?**
A: Yes, all vehicles have detailed inspection reports. You can also arrange physical inspection.

**Q: What if the vehicle doesn't match the description?**

**Q: Is financing available?**
