## Presentation Tips

### Design Guidelines
- Use consistent color scheme (brand colors)
- High-quality images and graphics
- Clean, professional layout
- Mobile-responsive if presenting digitally

### Delivery Tips
- Keep slides concise (6x6 rule: 6 bullets, 6 words each)
- Use storytelling approach
- Engage with questions
- Show platform demo when possible
- Prepare for Q&A

### Customization
- Adjust based on audience (investors vs. partners vs. users)
- Include relevant statistics for target market
- Highlight specific features based on interest
- Prepare backup slides for detailed questions

### Timing
- Full presentation: 20-30 minutes
- Quick pitch: 10-15 minutes
- Elevator pitch: 2-3 minutes
