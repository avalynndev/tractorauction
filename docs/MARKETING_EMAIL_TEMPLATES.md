## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips
- Keep it under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly
- Create urgency when appropriate
- Personalize when possible
- A/B test different variations

### Content Guidelines
- Mobile-responsive design
- Clear call-to-action buttons
- Personalization tokens
- Unsubscribe link
- Plain text version available

### Timing
- Welcome emails: Immediately
- Transactional: Real-time
- Promotional: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM
- Newsletter: First Tuesday of month
- Re-engagement: After 30 days of inactivity

### Segmentation
- By user type (Buyer/Seller/Dealer/OEM)
- By activity level
- By location
- By preferences
- By purchase history
